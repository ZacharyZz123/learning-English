2024年中考英语高分写作替换词+万能句式+万能套用模板
高分写作替换词
1. 有用的
普通词汇：useful
高分替换词：valuable
例句：Thank you for your valuable advice.
2. 总而言之
普通词：in a word
高分替换词：generally speaking, in conclusion, in general, in short, on the whole, all in all
例句：In conclusion,we are supposed to takeaction to protect the environment.
3. 事实上
普通词：in fact
高分替换词：as a matter of fact, actually, the truth is, in truth
例句：Actually, we can’t succeed in everything we try. What’s important is that we should stick to it.
4. 导致
普通词：cause
高分替换词：lead to, result in
例句：They say large crowds of tourists to the zoo will result in traffic jams.
5. 参加
普通词：join
高分替换词：take part in, participate in, attend
例句：We should try our best to take part in outdoor activities.
6. 知道，意识到，认识到
普通词：know
高分替换词：realize
例句：More and morepeople realize the importance of keeping healthy.
